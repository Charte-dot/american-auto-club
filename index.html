Welcome

To The Authentic American Auto Club website. We are absolutly nuts and bolts about American muscle cars big and small and are located in Dublin Ireland. We hope you enjoy browsing around our site. Sit back with a cuppa and come see what we are all about.


From our galley filled with pictures of our members cars to our events calendar, there is plenty to see. We also have a membership sign up if you would like to join our club.


New members always welcome, from new American car owners to the dreamers of one day owning an American muscle car. If you have an enquiries check out our contact page for more information.
