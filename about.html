About Us

The Authentic American Auto Club was established in 2000, by a group of friends who grew up watching Knight Rider, Dukes of hazzard and A team. Finally after years of dreaming we saved money together and bought a 1969 Ford Mustang. Our club was now on the way to bigger and better times. With the celtic tiger era in full swing in 2002-2007, populaity of importing American muscle cars grew. By 2004 we had 50 members and today we have 275 members worldwide.


With a purpose bought property near Baldonnel Airbase in 2003, we now had a place to call our own and build the club up from the ground. We currently have indoor and outdoor show grounds, bathroom facilities, kitchette to make tea and coffee and all areas are wheelchair friendly.


As our club continues to grow, we decided we wanted to give back to the community.
So every year we choose a charity partner. We fund raise with a charity car run, donate the admission fees from the car show and have a gigantic raffle on the day in aid of our charity partner.

This year we are partnered with Bumblance. A charity for sick children, that helps these children travel in comfort to and from hospital. Please check out the link below for more information.
